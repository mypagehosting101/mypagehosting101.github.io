Our Story

A Decade of Shaping Young Minds in Sangotedo

For over ten years, Recorn Nursery & Primary School has been a trusted name in early childhood and primary education in Sangotedo, Lagos. What started as a vision to provide affordable yet quality education has blossomed into an institution that rivals the city's premium schools — without the prohibitive price tag.

We combine a rigorous academic curriculum with strong moral values, ensuring every child develops not just intellectually, but in character and confidence. Our motto, "Godliness and Diligence," is woven into everything we do.

Programmes

Designed for Every Stage of Growth

From their earliest years to primary school graduation, we provide age-appropriate learning pathways that inspire curiosity and build strong foundations.

🧒

Nursery School

A warm, play-based learning environment for ages 2–5 where curiosity leads the way. Children develop social, motor, and early literacy skills through guided exploration.

Ages 2–5 Play-Based Phonics
📖

Primary School

A structured, engaging programme for ages 6–11 covering core academics, sciences, and social studies. We prepare pupils for Common Entrance and beyond.
